## Ideas Reviewed from Lab 1
| Idea                | Problem Area                                                                                        | Target User                                              | Current Alternative                                                                                          | Initial Technology Direction                                                            |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| HallPass            | International students lack honest dorm/cafeteria reviews and basic settling-in information         | New international students                               | Asking friends, Facebook groups, Google searches, and university websites that may be outdated or incomplete | Web app + cloud-based storage for reviews and user data                                 |
| CampusMarket        | No safe, student-only platform to buy and sell second-hand items                                    | Budget-conscious students                                | Facebook Marketplace, Carousell, and public buy/sell groups without student verification                     | Web app + cloud-based storage for listings and user accounts (university email sign-up) |

| Lost & Found Portal | Students frequently lose personal belongings and have no central place to report or search for them | University students and staff                            | Facebook groups, LINE chats, campus notice boards, and word of mouth                                         | Web app + cloud-based storage for lost/found item reports and user accounts             |
| Campus Event Hub    | Students miss campus events because information is spread across multiple platforms                 | University students, clubs, and university organizations | Posters, social media pages, group chats, and word of mouth                                                  | Web app + cloud-based storage for event information, registrations, and user accounts   |
| Campus FAQ Hub      | New students have difficulty finding reliable answers about campus life, services, and procedures   | New university students                                  | Asking seniors, university websites, social media groups, and student forums                                 | Web app + searchable cloud-based knowledge base for FAQs and student guides             |
