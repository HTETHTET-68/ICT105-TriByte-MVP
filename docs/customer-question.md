# HallPass Customer Validation Survey

This survey is conducted by **Team TriByte** as part of **ICT105 Fundamental Technology Entrepreneurship** at **Rangsit International College**.

The purpose of this survey is to understand how students find information about campus life such as dorms and cafeterias when they first arrive at university.

* This survey takes about 3 to 5 minutes to complete.
* Your responses are anonymous and will only be used for academic purposes.
* There are no right or wrong answers.
* Participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time.

By continuing this survey, you agree to participate.
